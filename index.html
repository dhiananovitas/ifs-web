Our Product

NUTRISOIL is a Special Organic Fertilizer that contain Adjustable Macro Nutrient (Nitrogen, Phosphates and Potassium). The appearance of this product are as follows :
  • Granules shaped.
  • ø size 2-5 mm.
  • The colour is dark grey.

Nutrisoil C+

NUTRISOIL C+

Indonesia Fertilizer Supplier's main product is NUTRISOIL C+. This product is an organic fertilizer with organic matter content more than 60% and have Macro Nutrient Nitrogen 4%, Phosphate 2% and Potassium 1%. It is our MAIN PRODUCT, because capable to be used in almost all lind of PLANTS.

Nutrisoil NPK+

NUTRISOIL NPK+

Besides C+, we can also make product with special specifications. We called it NUTRISOIL NPK+. We can arrange the composition of macro nutrient NPK, amount of organic content, level of solubility and combine it with chemical fertilizer. For example, there are some specifications of products that we ever produced :
  • NPK 4-3-3 organic matter 50%
  • NPK 5-5-5 organic matter 50%
  • NPK 9-8-7 organic matter 40%
  • NPK 10-5-6 organic matter 45%

Milestone

1997

The building established in 1997 as foreign investment company in Indonesia. Our location is at Jalan Bangil - Sukorejo KM. 04 Desa Oro-Oro Ombo Wetan, Kecamatan Rembang, Kabupaten Pasuruan, East Java, INdonesia. It took only 1 hour from the second bug city in Indonesia, Surabaya.

2011

Indonesia Fertilizer Supplier founded in 2011. The South Korean investor and fertilizer expert bring their technology to produce high quality fertilizer in Indonesia and the company starter exporting its products to Korea.

2015

By 2015, the company is acquired by local investors and combines knowledge and technology from Korea with trained human and management resources from Indonesia to improve effectiveness and efficiency in producing high quality and better products. This factory supplies all their product to fulfil organic fertilizer demand in Korea.

2017

In 2017, we began to expand the market to local and overseas beside Korea. We add more variations on product types as well.
